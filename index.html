About

Tempest Gaming is the newest Indie Game Studio on the market and we are dedicated to creating the most immersive, mesmerizing, and entertaining gaming experiences for everyone with new and unique takes on old classic genres like 2-D action games, JRPG's, and more! We draw a considerable amount of our inspiration around legendary games like Zelda, the Final Fantasy series, Metroid, Super Mario Bros., and so many more! These games defined a generation and inspired a culture change and we're here to bring back some of that lost magic!
